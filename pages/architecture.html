SILENCE IN THE CITY



Sometimes, the signage system of a city can confuse you. Imagine a city without all these indications, where buildings, homes, trees will be themselves those signage.

Thanks to this set of photomontages, I mix together elements from our cities.

I try to transpose the feeling of an architectural chaos
that we can currently found in cities where we do not have
any point of reference. However, the fact of seperate architectural elements can be read into a new way to inform directions.
