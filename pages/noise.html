noisy noise



Across this edition, I have searched how to graphically
show the sound. Thanks to different type of paper, more
or less thick, I have designed three sorts of sounds.

The first are the ones from outdoor, that I can not escape,
like the noise pollution of cars. The second are those come
from a close room, muffled by walls. And the last are those from the music I have chosen to listen during the time of this project.

The temporality of the sound is interrogated, between
a sharp noise or a steady sound. The opacity of some texture, pixels of a photograph or geometric shapes are put in parallel with the feeling of all those sounds that make my everyday life.
