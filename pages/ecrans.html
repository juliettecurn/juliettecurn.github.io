ECRANS VARIABLES



I have created this series of posters for the "Ecrans Variables" festival. It is an independent cinema festival organised by the Rennes 2 university.

Each posters is designed on the same way : they detailled the line up and showed a photo of a famous film scene. All those elements are organised thanks to simple shapes as squares, zigzags which represent all the movement and dynamic around the festival.
