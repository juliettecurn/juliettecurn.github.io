écrans variables



I created this serie of posters for the "Ecrans Variables" festival. It is a independent cinema festival organised by the Rennes 2 university.
Each posters are designed on the same way : they detailled the line up and showed a photo of a famous film scene. All those elements are organised thanks to simple shapes as square, zigzags which represent all movement and dynamic around the festival.



March, 2016
